A downtown Lincoln event venue with "rustic urban charm"

The Talon Room (Formerly, Warehouse No. 4) has been established in downtown Lincoln as an event venue with “rustic urban charm.” The building features distressed brick walls, exposed industrial ceilings, and a unique antique bar to provide your event a truly unique setting.

We go above and beyond to create events that will leave a mark.

Whether you are searching for a place to have your first dance as a married couple, celebrate an anniversary, thank your hard working employees with a company party, tailgate for Big Ten games, or even host a Greek life event, you can be sure everyone will have an excellent time in the Talon Room!
