Wine-Down Wednesday

$20.00 per person

The price is $20 per person purchased prior to the event or $25 at the door

“Wine-Down Wednesday” is a monthly wine tasting created in part with the “Talon Room Tasting” series. “Talon Room Tastings” are exclusive events held at the Talon Room available to the public. The Talon Room is featuring 5 wines from Italian Vine: Wine Distribution and 5 paired appetizers from ChefauChef catering. Partnered with Mark McDonald from Italian Vine, guests will get a chance to taste some fine wines, learn a little about the wines and have a lot of fun.

All wines featured will be available for purchase through the Talon Room. Many of these wines are not available for purchase around town.

Note: Items are not currently available for purchase through website. For more information, please fill out a contact info or book tour form. All events will be assessed a $500 non-refundable security deposit required at the time of booking and will be credited towards the final bill.
